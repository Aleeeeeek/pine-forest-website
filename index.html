Patient-Focused Medical Assessments

We provide a holistic and patient-focused approach to your health, that simultaneously addresses the physical, mental, emotional, social, and spiritual components of health.

Our comprehensive assessments include:

  • Complete physical examination
  • Mental health evaluation
  • Emotional wellness assessment
  • Social support evaluation
  • Spiritual well-being consideration

Each assessment is tailored to your individual needs, ensuring a complete understanding of your health status and providing actionable recommendations for your well-being.

×

Independent Medical Evaluators

Our independent medical evaluators provide thorough, unbiased assessments for legal, insurance, and disability claims.

Our evaluation services include:

  • Legal case medical evaluations
  • Insurance claim assessments
  • Disability determination evaluations
  • Workers' compensation assessments
  • Expert medical opinions and reports

All evaluations are conducted by qualified healthcare professionals with extensive experience in medical-legal assessments, ensuring accuracy, objectivity, and compliance with regulatory standards.

Application for CAT Determination

We assist with applications for Catastrophic (CAT) Determination under Ontario's statutory accident benefits.

Our CAT determination services include:

  • Comprehensive medical assessments
  • Detailed impairment evaluations
  • Complete documentation preparation
  • Expert medical reporting
  • Support throughout the application process

Our team understands the complexity of CAT determinations and works diligently to provide thorough, accurate assessments that support your application process.
