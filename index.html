Patient-Focused Medical Assessments

A comprehensive, person-centered approach to your well-being

The Ontario Independent Assessment Centre provides a broad range of objective medical evaluations designed to support the resolution of insurance-related matters. Our services cover Accident Benefits, Medical-Legal Tort cases, and Catastrophic Impairment assessments, along with specialized support for Malpractice cases.

Our experienced clinicians perform detailed evaluations across multiple disciplines, including:

  • In-Home & Occupational Therapy Assessments
  • Attendant Care Needs Analysis
  • Worksite & Ergonomic Evaluations
  • Orthopedic Examinations
  • Chronic Pain Review & Treatment Recommendations
  • Cognitive Function Assessments
  • Functional Abilities Evaluations
  • Psychological Assessment & Therapy Planning
  • Driver Reintegration Assessment & Rehabilitation
  • Biopsychosocial Assessment & Follow-up
  • Neurological Assessment
  • Concussion Evaluation & Treatment
  • Psychiatric Assessment
  • Neuropsychological Review
  • Vocational Assessment
  • TMJ Examination
  • ENT Evaluation
  • Physiatry Consultation
  • Psycho-Vocational Assessment
  • Optometry Evaluation
  • Neuro-Optometry Evaluation
  • Nutritional Assessment
  • Plastic Surgery Assessment
  • Speech-Language Pathology Reviews
  • Future Care Cost Reports
  • Capacity Evaluations
  • Medical-Legal Assessments for TORT Files

Beyond assessments, OIAC supports clients with access to assistive medical devices and rental options such as hospital beds, wheelchairs, and walkers. We also arrange transportation and provide translation services to ensure every appointment is smooth, accessible, and stress-free.

Application for CAT Determination

Detailed assessments for life-impacting conditions

The Ontario Independent Assessment Centre (OIAC) has built its reputation on delivering high-quality medical evaluations. With the expansion of our CAT Determination services, we offer a thorough and structured assessment process that meets the highest standards in the field.

Our approach ensures every case is reviewed with precision, transparency, and care. We understand the significant implications of catastrophic impairment determinations, and our certified assessors work meticulously to provide accurate and defensible reports. From the initial consultation to the final documentation, our focus is on clarity, professionalism, and consistency.
